E Resource Platform
The Resource Management Platform E Resource of the Internet of Things is developed independently by students of the Internet of Things Engineering Department.
The platform is used for sharing resources such as articles, pictures, project works, etc., as well as showing teachers and students' works.

Resource management

This platform provides convenient resource management functions. Here you can view the resources (PPT, Examination, Project, Software) resources and article resources of the Institute of Internet of Things, and download the resources for free after logging in; as long as you have corresponding Permissions, any one can upload resources here.

Authority mechanism

We give it an ability to perform every operation on this platform, and only those with this ability can perform the corresponding operations. The system adopts the privilege fragmentation design, which can customize the role and assign permissions to the user arbitrarily, which is very convenient for management personnel to use.

Resource protection

The platform adopts stateless identity authentication. Any operation of any user is visible to the administrator, so there is no need to worry about the deleted resources being deleted or tampered with, and the resources need to be authenticated. It is also very impressive for the security of the system. The platform has anti-DOS attack, anti-SQL injection, anti-XSS attack and other functions. For these malicious attackers, we will have corresponding measures to prevent it.
